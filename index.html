Gongs Vegetable Stir-Fry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, broccoli, snow peas)
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ginger, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and ginger to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add mixed vegetables to the skillet. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil.
  5. Pour the sauce mixture over the vegetables in the skillet. Stir well to coat all the vegetables evenly.
  6. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens slightly.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Remove from heat and serve hot.
